Once you activate [DDoS Protection Unmetered](/en/documentation/products/edge-firewall/ddos-protection/), you'll be protected against DDoS attacks. The protections works with unmetered mitigation and it automatically mitigates attacks against your domains and applications.

:::note
DDos Protection Unmetered is availabe for clients with **Business**, **Enterprise**, and **Mission Critical** Service Plans.
:::

You can activate this module at any point of your journey. It'll be helpful to protect your applications against volumetrics attacks as it has the always-on intelligence to mitigate complex attacks.

It doesn't require any configurations, only that you activate it in your account and in a firewall:

1. Access Real-Time Manager (RTM) > [select the Account menu](/en/documentation/products/guides/how-to-access-rtm/#managing-the-account).
2. Select **Billing & Subscriptions** > **Subscriptions**.
3. In the **Edge Firewall** section, find **DDoS Protection Unmetered** and enable the switch.

Next, go to the [edge firewall](/en/documentation/products/guides/secure/configure-main-settings#modules) in which you want to activate DDoS Protection Unmetered:

1. Select an edge firewall.
2. Enable the **DDoS Protection Unmetered** switch.

Now, the domains associated with the specific edge firewall will be protected against DDoS attacks.

You can activate the DDoS module in any edge firewall you desire, as well as use it along with other modules.
