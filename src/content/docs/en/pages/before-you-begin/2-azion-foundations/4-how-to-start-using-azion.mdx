To start using Azion, you must [create a new account](/en/documentation/products/accounts/creating-account/) via **Real-Time Manager (RTM)**.

The [Get started](/en/documentation/products/get-started/) documentation provides information to guide you in your first steps: getting to know the platform, building your first application, completing a few configurations, and checking your metrics.

You can also **Choose your journey** and explore the products or features of [Azion categories](/en/documentation/products/azion-foundations/azion-products-catalog/):

- [Build](/en/documentation/products/guides/build/overview/)
- [Secure](link secure overview)
- [Deploy](link deploy overview)
- [Observe](link observe overview)

Or review the [Developer tools](/en/documentation/products/dev-tools/) and find the one that adapts to you better.

:::tip
If you're a company looking for a Business or higher support plan, [contact the Azion Experts](https://www.azion.com/en/contact-sales/). They can assess, plan, and implement solutions according to your needs, helping you to adopt Azion Edge Platform faster.
:::

### More on Azion

You can also explore other resources to learn further about **Azion Edge Platform**.

- Understand your account: explore how your Azion account works and how to set up the main configurations.
- [Azion Roadmap](https://azion.com/en/roadmap/): find out the latest launched products and features and the projects Azion is working on.
- [System status](https://status.azion.com/): check the status and availability of Azion's network, platform, and services.
- [Get help](/en/documentation/products/get-help/): discover the available tools and channels to get help at any point in your Azion journey.
- **Join the community**: find news about Azion, seek advice, and share experiences on [Discord](https://discord.gg/Yp9N7RMVZy), [Medium](https://medium.com/aziontech), [GitHub](https://github.com/aziontech), [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/aziontech/), [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/aziontech), and [X/Twitter](https://twitter.com/aziontech).
