[Azion Console](https://console.azion.com) is the configuration interface where you can access all the available products and complete all tasks in Azion's platform. 

This documentation will assist you to access the interface and explore the products and resources.

## Logging in

To log in, you must go to the [Azion Console](https://console.azion.com) and use your access credentials.

The platform will ask you for the **email** and **password** that you registered when creating your account.
- If you don't have an account yet, create a new one by visiting the [sign-up page](https://console.azion.com/signup).

Once you've completed this process, the Console homepage will open.

:::tip
Enable [Multi⁠-⁠Factor Authentication](/en/documentation/products/accounts/multi-factor-authentication/) for an extra layer of security while accessing your account. Check the guide on [how to enable MFA](/en/documentation/products/guides/multi-factor-authentication/) for more details.
:::

## Using the shortcuts

The homepage has different **cards** that work as **shortcuts** to open certain products or start some tasks.

If there's a card with the product or task you need, just click on it and it'll redirect you to the desired page.

---

## Selecting a product

To access all the available products and their settings:

1. Click the *three horizontal lines* located on the upper-left corner to open the **Products menu**.
2. Explore the sections to find the product you're looking for.
3. Click on the desired option to be redirected to the product.

The **Products menu** starts with the following labels:

- **Home** returns you to the homepage.
- **Marketplace** opens Azion's digital catalog.
- **Domains** opens the domain generation and management service.

Other products and services are organized into the following sections:

- **Build**: has **Applications** and **Variables**.
- **Secure**: includes **Edge DNS** and **Firewall**.
- **Deploy**: has **Edge Nodes**.
- **Observe**: includes **Data Stream**, **Edge Pulse**, **Real-Time Metrics**, and **Real-Time Events**.
- **Tools**: has **Real-Time Purge**.
- **Edge Libraries**: includes **Certificate Manager**, **Edge Services**, **Functions**, **Network Lists**, and **WAF Rules**.

## Managing the account

To access your account and edit your settings:

1. Click the *avatar icon* located on the upper-right corner to open the **Account menu**.
2. Explore the sections to find the option you're looking for.
3. Click on the desired option to be redirected to that page.

The **Account menu** has your account information and the following options: **Account Settings**, **Users Management**, **Billing**, **Activity History**, **Teams Permissions**, **Your Settings**, **Personal Token**, and **Theme**.
  - If you have an **Enterprise plan** and and your account has *Account Owner* status, you'll find the option to access [SSO Management](/en/documentation/products/guides/sso/) in this section.
- The second section should have your profile's name as label. Here, you can access your **Settings**, **Personal Tokens** ,and **Theme** to choose dark or light mode.
- Finally, the **Logout** label will finish your session clicking on it.
