:::caution[important]
Azion has two user interfaces: Real-Time Manager and Console, which is in *Preview* stage. Currently, Console is only available for Developer plans and new accounts. Follow the steps according to the user interface you're using.
:::

To begin using Azion Edge Platform and all the available products, you need to complete the sign-up process and log in to the configuration interface.

You can use two methods to create your Azion account: *email* and *Social Login*. The next sections will provide more information on the sign-up process.

---

## Creating an account using your email address

To create an account with your email address, proceed as follows:

<Tabs client:visible>
    <Fragment slot="tab.console">Console</Fragment>
    <Fragment slot="tab.rtm">Real-Time Manager</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="panel.console">
1. On the [sign-up page](https://console.azion.com/signup), click the **Sign Up with Work Email** button; new fields will open.
2. Fill in the following required fields:
    - **Work Email**
    - **Password**

- You automatically agree to the [Customer Agreement Terms of Service](/en/documentation/agreements/customer-agreement/) and [Privacy Policy](/en/documentation/agreements/privacy-policy/) by proceeding with the registration process.

3. Click the **Sign Up** button.

To confirm your account creation, you must verify your email. Check your inbox and look for the verification email.

:::note
In the message, you'll find instructions to confirm your email address. Click the URL link to activate your account. This link is only valid for *24 hours*.

If you don't receive the email, check your *Spam* folder.

If you have any questions, [contact the sales team](https://www.azion.com/en/contact-sales/).
:::

By clicking on the link sent by email, the [Azion Console](https://console.azion.com) page will open. To sign in:

1. Enter your email and click **Next**.
2. Enter your password and click the **Sign In** button. 
3. Complete the form to personalize your experience, according to your preferences.
4. Click the **Submit** button.

You're now able to access **Azion Console** and start building your edge applications. Check the [Get Started](/en/documentation/products/get-started/) page to continue your onboarding journey.
</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="panel.rtm">
1. On the [sign-up page](https://manager.azion.com/signup), click the **Sign up with email** button; a new form will open.
2. Fill in the following required fields:
    - **Full name**
    - **E-mail**
    - **Password**

- You automatically agree to the [Customer Agreement Terms of Service](/en/documentation/agreements/customer-agreement/) and [Privacy Policy](/en/documentation/agreements/privacy-policy/) by proceeding with the registration process.

3. Click the **Sign up with email** button.

To confirm your account creation, you must verify your email. Check your inbox and look for the verification email.

:::note
In the message, you'll find instructions to confirm your email address. Click the URL link to activate your account. This link is only valid for *24 hours*.

If you don't receive the email, check your *Spam* folder.

If you have any questions, [contact the sales team](https://www.azion.com/en/contact-sales/).
:::

By clicking on the link sent by email, the [Real-Time Manager](https://manager.azion.com/) page will open. To sign in:

1. Enter your email and click **Proceed**.
2. Enter your password and click the **Sign in** button. 
3. Complete the form to personalize your experience, according to your preferences.
4. Click the **Create my account** button.

You're now able to access **Azion RTM** and start building your edge applications. Check the [Get Started](/en/documentation/products/get-started/) page to continue your onboarding journey.
</Fragment>

</Tabs>

## Creating an account using Social Login

Azion also allows you to create your account through Social Login. Currently, the platform supports **GitHub** and **Google** accounts.

To create an account using a Social Login, proceed as follows:

<Tabs client:visible>
    <Fragment slot="tab.console">Console</Fragment>
    <Fragment slot="tab.rtm">Real-Time Manager</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="panel.console">
1. From the [sign-up page](https://console.azion.com/signup), click the *Social Login* option of your choice:

- **Continue with GitHub**
- **Continue with Google**

2. If requested, enter your username and password according to the chosen *Social Login* option.

   - In some cases, you'll need to complete a few more steps, such as proceeding with authorization options.

3. Complete the form to personalize your experience, according to your preferences.
4. Click the **Submit** button.

By choosing to create your account via Social Login, there's no need to verify your email.

You're now able to access **Console** and start building your edge applications. Check the [Get Started](/en/documentation/products/get-started/) page to continue your onboarding journey.
</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="panel.rtm">
1. From the [sign-up page](https://manager.azion.com/signup), click the *Social Login* option of your choice:

- **Continue with GitHub**
- **Continue with Google**

2. If requested, enter your username and password according to the chosen *Social Login* option.

   - In some cases, you'll need to complete a few more steps, such as proceeding with authorization options.

3. Complete the form to personalize your experience, according to your preferences.
4. Click the **Create my account** button.

By choosing to create your account via Social Login, there's no need to verify your email.

You're now able to access **RTM** and start building your edge applications. Check the [Get Started](/en/documentation/products/get-started/) page to continue your onboarding journey.
</Fragment>

</Tabs>
