**Billing** provides a comprehensive view of your usage-based pricing model.

<Button href="/en/documentation/products/accounts/billing-and-subscriptions/" label="Go to Billing reference" type="primary" theme="light" target="_blank" />

To access **Billing**:

1. Access [Azion Console](https://console.azion.com).
2. On the upper-right corner of the page, select the avatar menu. This is the **Account menu**.
3. Select **Billing**.

You'll find the **Bills** and **Payment Methods** tabs.

---

## Bills

The **Bills** section includes:

- **Current Invoice**, showing the billing period and all charges on your account.
    - By clicking the **Details** button, you can open the **Invoice details**, which explain each charge.
- **Service Plan**, displaying your active plan, payment method, currency, and credit balance.

---

## Payment Methods

This section displays all the payment methods you've associated with your account.

To add a new payment method:

1. Click the **+ Payment Method** button.
2. On **Card Holder Name**, write the name of the owner of the credit card.
3. On **Card Number**, add the number of the credit card.
4. On **Expiration Date**, add the date of expiration exhibited on your credit card.
5. On **Security code (CVC/CVV)**, add the number exhibited on your credit card.
6. Click the **Save** button.

Alternatively, you can click the **+ Credit** button to define a specific amount to be credited to your account.

To set a payment method as default, still on the list page:

1. Click the three dots button located on the right side of the registered payment method.
2. Select the **Set as default**. 

Now, this payment method will be used automatically for future transactions.

---

## Payment History

The **Payment History** appears in the **Bills** tab. 

In this section, you can find more information about the charges and filter them for detailed viewing. For example: payment date, transactions amount, payment method, and invoice.
