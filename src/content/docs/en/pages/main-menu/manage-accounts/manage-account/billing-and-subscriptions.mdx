**Billing and Subscriptions** provides a comprehensive view of your usage-based pricing model, as well as the products your account is subscribed to.

<LinkButton link="/en/documentation/products/accounts/billing-and-subscriptions/" label="Go to Billing reference" severity="secondary" target="_blank" />

To access **Billing and Subscriptions**:

<Tabs client:visible>
    <Fragment slot="tab.console">Console</Fragment>
    <Fragment slot="tab.rtm">Real-Time Manager</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="panel.console">
1. Access [Azion Console](https://console.azion.com).
2. On the upper-right corner of the page, select the avatar menu. This is the **Account menu**.
3. Select **Billing & Subscriptions**.

You'll find the **Bills** and **Payment Methods** tabs.
</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="panel.rtm">
1. Access [Real-Time Manager (RTM)](https://manager.azion.com/).
2. On the upper-right corner of the page, select the avatar menu. This is the **Account menu**.
3. Select **Billing & Subscriptions**.

You'll find four tabs: **Bills**, **Payment Methods**, **Payment History**, and **Subscriptions**.
</Fragment>

</Tabs>

## Bills

<Tabs client:visible>
    <Fragment slot="tab.console">Console</Fragment>
    <Fragment slot="tab.rtm">Real-Time Manager</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="panel.console">
The **Bills** section includes:

- **Current Invoice**, showing the billing period and all charges on your account.
    - By clicking the **Details** button, you can open the **Invoice details**, which explain each charge.
- **Service Plan**, displaying your active plan, payment method, currency, and credit balance.
</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="panel.rtm">
To view the charges of your account:

1. Under **Month**, select the dropdown menu.
2. Choose the month/year from which you want to view charges.
3. Click the **Filter** button.
</Fragment>

</Tabs>

## Payment Methods

<Tabs client:visible>
    <Fragment slot="tab.console">Console</Fragment>
    <Fragment slot="tab.rtm">Real-Time Manager</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="panel.console">
This section displays all the payment methods you've associated with your account.

To add a new payment method:

1. Click the **+ Payment Method** button.
2. On **Card Holder Name**, write the name of the owner of the credit card.
3. On **Card Number**, add the number of the credit card.
4. On **Expiration Date**, add the date of expiration exhibited on your credit card.
5. On **Security code (CVC/CVV)**, add the number exhibited on your credit card.
6. Click the **Save** button.

Alternatively, you can click the **+ Credit** button to define a specific amount to be credited to your account.

To set a payment method as default, still on the list page:

1. Click the three dots button located on the right side of the registered payment method.
2. Select the **Set as default**. 

Now, this payment method will be used automatically for future transactions.
</Fragment>

## Subscriptions

> Currently, this section is only available in RTM.

You'll find seven sections: **Edge Application**, **Edge Firewall**, **Edge Functions**, **Edge Routing**, **Edge Analytics**, **Edge Orchestration**, and **Support**.

To subscribe to a product, enable the switch next to the desired product.

To unsubscribe to a product, disable the switch next to the desired product.
